ID Crisis

In many cases, I have taken traditional words and phrases associated with queer identity and given them new meanings. For example, in the painting 'I'm Not Gay, I'm Just a Sissy', the word 'sissy' is used to subvert traditional ideas about gender and sexuality.

Another example is in the painting 'No Homo', which uses a slang term typically used by straight men to reassure each other that they are not gay. In this painting, the term is used to express the homophobic phonomenon.

I hope you enjoy these artworks and that they help you to think about queer identity in new and exciting ways.
