Adrift

In love

Love is a shipwreck
and we are the survivors
washed up on a desert island
with only each other
to keep us company

We build a shelter from the sun
and spend our days exploring
the island we call home

At night we lie in each other's arms
and watch the stars
wondering if anyone is looking for us

We are content to be
adrift in love
until the end of time

In life

We're all just adrift in life
Trying to find our way
Each of us searching
For something to hold on to
Something to make us feel alive
We're all just drifting
Lost in the sea of life
Looking for a sign
That will lead us to shore
But sometimes we get caught
In the currents and pulled
Under by the weight of our own lives
And we can't find our way back up
We just keep treading water
Trying to stay afloat
And hoping that someday
We'll find land again

In the world

I am adrift in the world,
But content in my inner world.

There is so much turmoil and strife,
But I find peace within myself.

I am content to be alone,
In my own little world.

It is a world of my own making,
And it is all I need.
