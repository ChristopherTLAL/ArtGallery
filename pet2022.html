Pet

The watercolour gallery presents a series of portraits of all the pets I encountered in Cambridge. Each pet is shown in a different setting, with a different background and different colours.
P.S. The panda is by no means from Cambridge.

Cute one at Christ’s Piece

This little ball of fluff
So full of life and love
Tugged at my heartstrings
The moment I laid eyes on her
She was so playful and carefree
Running and jumping around
Not a care in the world
I wished I could take her home
And give her all the love she deserves
But alas, she was not mine
And I had to walk away
Leaving her behind in Christ's piece
