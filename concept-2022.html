Inspired by Knowledge (not updated)

This set of artworks is inspired by linguistics, the study of language. It explores the relationship between words and meaning, and how language can be used to communicate ideas. The artwork uses a variety of linguistic devices to create a visual representation of the way language works. It is a playful and interactive exploration of the power of words, and how they can be used to create meaning.

Brain

The artwork is inspired by the two sides of human brain. The left side of the brain is responsible for logical thinking, while the right side is responsible for creative thinking. Also, I am thinking about how monolingual imperialism upholds the idea of 'semilingual' to discriminate the plurilingual speakers.

Floating

The artwork depicts a person floating in water, with a feeling of deprivation. He looks lost and alone. The water represents the scenario when you have to express yourself, and the person is struggling to keep their head above water.

Sad Cloud

The painting is a representation of how the artist sees the world; through the lens of social media and technology. The emoji in the clouds is a symbol of how our world is becoming more and more reliant on technology and how it is changing the way we communicate. The painting is a commentary on how we are losing touch with reality and our connection to the natural world.

My Thesis

The cat looks at the thesis with a sense of bemusement. It is clear that the cat does not understand the thesis, but it is also clear that the cat is not particularly bothered by this fact. The cat seems content to simply sit and stare at the thesis, perhaps pondering its own place in the universe.
