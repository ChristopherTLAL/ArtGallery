Body controlled by government

Our bodies are not our own,
To the government we're beholden.
They tell us what to do and where to go,
And we must obey or face the consequences.

They tell us what to eat and what to wear,
And how to think and how to feel.
We're just puppets on a string,
Dancing to the government's tune.

They control our every move,
And we can do nothing to stop them.
We're just pawns in their game,
And they'll use us however they please.

Pride Month!!!

In celebration of Pride month,
We come together as one,
To stand up for our rights,
And to show the world that love is love.

We march in the streets
With our colours flying high,
Showing the world that we're proud,
And that we're not going to hide.

We're fighting for equality,
And we're not going to stop,
Until every person is free
To love who they want to love.
