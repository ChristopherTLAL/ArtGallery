Boutique

The artworks depict beautiful natural scenes with vibrant colours and a strong sense of smell. I would like to remind us that we should take time to stop and smell the roses, and that the world is a beautiful place when we take the time to appreciate it.

Trip to Cambridge Botanic Garden

A day well spent exploring the beauty of nature with good company.
