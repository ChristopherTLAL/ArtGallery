Conflict

It can be frustrating to see the conflicts on the internet being boosted. The echo chamber effect is partly to blame, but also capitalism and political propaganda. My artworks presented the person who was baffled because of these different views, especially when they hold different points.

I am the conflict of self,
the battle within myself.
I am the one who is fighting,
and the one who is being fought.
I am the winner and the loser,
the victor and the vanquished.
I am the artist and the artwork,
the creator and the creation.
