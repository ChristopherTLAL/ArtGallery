Walking along river

The artwork is a representation of my experience of walking along River Cam and catching the sense of nature.
I wanted to create a piece that would capture the feeling of being in nature, and the peace that comes with it. I wanted the viewer to feel as though they are walking along the river with me, and to feel the sense of calm that comes with being in nature.
The artwork is a peaceful scene, with the river flowing calmly and the trees providing shade and shelter. The colours are soft and natural, and the overall effect is one of serenity.
