Longing

This set of paintings was made during my very emotional period at Cambridge, longing for the new life here while experiencing acute nostalgia.
The style of them is inspired by Van Gogh who painted approximately 70 oils with his ‘memories of the North’ during the last weeks at Saint-Rémy.

Get Started

I long for the days
When I could see the art
In all its beauty
And not just in my head.
I long for the days
When I could feel the brush
Against my skin
And the paint on my fingertips.
I long for the days
When I could smell the oil
And the turpentine
And the canvas would be new.
I long for the days
When I could touch the frames
And run my fingers
Over the glass.
I long for the days
When I could stand in front
Of a painting
And just stare.

Ever-dreamed neverland

There's a longing in my heart
for a place I've never been
I can see it in the stars
and feel it in the wind
I'm sitting in front of the college
and I can't help but think
of the home I've never known
and the life that I could have had
if only I'd made different choices
Now I'm stuck here in this place
far from where I belong
and all I can do is dream
of the day I'll finally go home

Sitting for nothing

Sitting at Cambridge,
longing for nothing,
I watch the world go by.
All around me people hurry,
busy with their lives.
And I just sit and wonder,
what it is I'm missing.
Is it the excitement of the city,
or the peace of the countryside?
Sitting at Cambridge,
longing for nothing.
